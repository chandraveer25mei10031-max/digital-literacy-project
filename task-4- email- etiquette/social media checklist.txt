social media checklist
Responsible social media use as a college student
WE CAN DO:
1.Think before you post (avoid harmful or inappropriate content)
 2.Protect your privacy (don’t share personal information)
3.Use respectful language (no hate or bullying)
4. Avoid wasting too much time (manage screen time)
5.Share useful and positive content.

WE CAN`T DO :
1.Don’t accept friend requests from strangers
2.Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown messages
3. Don’t use weak or same passwords everywhere
4. Don’t share others’ photos/videos without permission
5. Don’t ignore privacy settings (keep them updated)
