  stay safe  online prevention checklist :
1. Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone.
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
3. Always verify UPI payment requests before accepting them.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
6. Keep your mobile and apps updated regularly.
7. don't o suspicious website.
8. Do not download apps from unknown sources.

If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately at:
National Cyber Crime Portal(cybercrime.gov.in)
Helpline Number: 1930
